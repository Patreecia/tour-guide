Welcome to Lagos State

The Centre of Excellence, you can probably call it a city of hustlers (Hustle and Bustle) or like Lagosians would describe it "A city that never Sleeps"

You cannot come to Nigeria and not go through Lagos State, enjoy a little sneak peak of what Lagos has got to offer

Top Three places to visit at Lagos

Nike Art Gallery

One of Nigeria's most important artists, Nike Davies-Okundaye.

runs this enormous gallery full of contemporary and traditional.

Nigerian arts

Badagry

The Badagry Black Heritage Museum holds hundreds

of artifacts and historical relics on slavery.

Lekki Conservation Center

This 21-year-old conservation center is a must vist, you will have a

time of your life discovering the nature park and appreciating

plants and animals in their natural habitant

Local delicacies to try out

You need to try our award wining dish in all

its glory.

Egusi soup with Semo

In Nigeria we have various indigenious delicies
